# Positioning

**Status:** Active market framing
**Authority:** Product positioning; not architecture
**Owner:** Founding Team
**Governing reference:** [ARCHITECTURE_FREEZE.md](../architecture/ARCHITECTURE_FREEZE.md)

## Category

Verification infrastructure for software development.

## Problem

Software can compile, deploy, and still fail to fulfill the promises developers
and users depend upon. As AI-generated changes increase the volume and
complexity of software, review by intuition becomes less reliable.

## Product

The platform discovers application capabilities, represents their Promises,
orchestrates deterministic or explicitly observational Proofs, preserves
Evidence, and proposes Repairs that must be re-verified.

## Differentiation

- **Evidence over opinion:** verdicts trace to retained Evidence.
- **One engine:** CLI, agents, editors, GitHub, and cloud share semantics.
- **Local first:** source and secrets remain local by default.
- **Capabilities over frameworks:** the model survives ecosystem churn.
- **Providers as plugins:** integrations do not contaminate core.
- **Structured by default:** machine interfaces are first-class.

## Not positioned as

- a test runner;
- a CI vendor;
- an observability or monitoring service;
- a deployment platform;
- an AI code generator;
- a dashboard that becomes a second source of truth.
